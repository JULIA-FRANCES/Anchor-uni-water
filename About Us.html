ABOUT US

Our Story

Anchor Uni Water Factory was esatablished in 2017 to provide clean, safe, and affordable water to the Anchor University community and its surrounding areas. Our journey began with a vision to address the water scarcity issues faced by the university and the Iyana-Ipaja community. Our VC Professor S.O Bandele gathered a team of dedicated individuals who shared the same vision and passion for providing quality water services. Heading this team was Mr. Tanimowo, who has been instrumental in the success of our operations. And now almost 8 years later, we have become a trusted source of water for our university and the whole Iyana-Ipaja Community.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver pure, safe, and affordable water to Anchor University and its surrounding communities. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of quality and service, ensuring that our customers receive the best possible experience. We strive to be a reliable source of clean water through continuous improvement, innovation, and sustainable practices. By prioritizing health, environmental responsibility, and community engagement, we aim to make a lasting positive impact on the lives of those we serve.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the leading water factory in Nigeria, renowned for our unwavering commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. We strive to set the industry standard by consistently delivering safe, clean, and refreshing drinking water that meets both national and international standards. As we grow, we are committed to empowering local communities, creating job opportunities, and promoting environmental stewardship through eco-friendly packaging and responsible water sourcing. With a customer-first approach and a drive for continuous improvement, we envision a future where our brand becomes a household name synonymous with health, trust, and reliability.
